John Cena

John Felix Anthony Cena (/ˈsiːnə/ SEE-nə; born April 23, 1977)[11] is an American actor and professional wrestler. As a wrestler, he has been signed to WWE since 2001, and is a record 16-time world champion as recognized by WWE.[a] He is widely regarded as one of the greatest professional wrestlers of all time.[12][13][14]
